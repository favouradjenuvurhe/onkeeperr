FAQ

What is MUCH WOW Token? 🤔

MUCH WOW Token is a community-driven meme coin built on the Ethereum blockchain. We combine the fun of memes with real utility, creating a vibrant ecosystem for our holders!

How do I buy MUCH WOW? 💎

You can buy MUCH WOW Token on Uniswap using ETH. Simply connect your wallet, set slippage to 5-6%, and swap ETH for MUCH WOW. Always verify the contract address before trading!

What are the tokenomics? 📊

Our tokenomics include 5% buy/sell tax (2% Marketing, 2% Liquidity, 1% Development), max wallet of 2%, and max transaction of 1%. Total supply is 1,000,000,000 tokens.

How can I join the community? 👥

Join our vibrant community on Telegram, Discord, and Twitter! We host daily meme contests, AMAs, and community events with amazing rewards!
